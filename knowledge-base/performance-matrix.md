# Performance Matrix

Format breakout rates sorted by 1M+ hit probability. Based on analysis of 1,176 Twitter/X posts.

---

## Format Rankings

| Format | 1M+ Rate | Best Structure | Worst Mistake |
|--------|----------|----------------|---------------|
| "Instead of Netflix" urgency | 50% | ALL CAPS + contrast (people who X / people who skip) | Explaining why the urgency is real |
| Relatable humor / workplace | 27% | "make no mistakes" / air-quotes / meme + caption | Explaining the joke |
| CEO quote + "cooked" | 22% | [Name:] + [blockquote] + [group] are cooked. | Paraphrasing instead of direct quote |
| Dialogue format | 3.3M median | Person asks, each answer escalates absurdity | Making questioner too knowledgeable |
| Smart posts w/ specific use cases | 12% | Specific numbers + personal result + consequence | Generic claims without specific use case |
| Tips / how-to | 12% | Numbered list + personal result | Generic prompts without use case |
| Product launch | 12% | "Instead of X, now Y" + "forever. read that again." | "Excited to announce" opener |
| Autonomous agent | 8% | Specific task list + "forever" + "read that again" | Summarizing without specific tasks |

---

## Key Finding: Volume vs Performance Inversion

Most-published format (developer/Claude Code, 34% of all posts) has the lowest ROI per post (67K median views).

Least-published formats have the highest breakout odds.

The formats with the smallest volume have the highest per-post performance. This means the agent should deliberately avoid the most common format and lean into underrepresented high-ROI formats.

---

## The Structure Lesson

The difference between 206K views and 7.4M views for the SAME information was three structural choices:

1. Direct quote vs paraphrase
2. Specific professions vs "white collar jobs"
3. Consequence closer ("cooked") vs no closer

Structure is the only variable. The information was identical. The framing created a 36x reach difference.

This is the single most important lesson: you cannot out-inform your way to virality. You can only out-structure.
