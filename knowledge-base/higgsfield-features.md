# Higgsfield Features

Full feature map organized by category.

---

## Core Video Generation

- **Seedance 2.0:** Full AI video generation. Text + image + video + audio input (up to 12 assets per generation). Native audio sync, character consistency, 15-second clips chainable. Elo 1,351 (#1 on Artificial Analysis image-to-video).
- **Cinema Studio 3.0:** Director-level control. Optical physics engine, 50+ camera presets, 3 simultaneous camera movements, virtual camera controls (sensor, lens, focal length). Post-production: temperature, contrast, saturation, grain, bloom, exposure.
- **Kling 3.0:** 15-second videos with character consistency. Native 4K at 60fps.
- **Sora 2:** 25-second native clips with narrative coherence.
- **WAN 2.5/2.6:** Video generation model.
- **MiniMax Hailuo 02:** Video generation.
- **Seedance Pro:** Premium tier.
