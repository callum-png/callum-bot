## POST 1: Bryce Hall — Polarizing Motivational + Product Discovery
**Performance:** Viral (Cal-confirmed)
**Format:** Motivational/confrontational with organic product mention

```
To the people who don't hate me, here is your chance to make it in life

Literally go create content and distribute it at scale

Money is literally waiting for you ( don't be fat n poor )
```

### Psychology Analysis (18 Rules):
- **Rule 1 (Confrontational opener): ✅** "To the people who don't hate me" — immediately creates in-group/out-group. Polarizing. You're either with Bryce or against him.
- **Rule 2 (Front-load value): ✅** "here is your chance to make it in life" — value promise in the first sentence
- **Rule 5 (Challenge closer): ✅** "don't be fat n poor" — attacks the reader's behavior directly. Guilt/motivation combo.
- **Rule 7 (Internet shorthand): ✅** "n" instead of "and", "ur" energy, casual caps
- **Rule 16 (Mass virality): ✅** Anyone understands this. No jargon. No niche. Pure attitude.
- **Rule 17 (Funny/shocking): ✅** "don't be fat n poor" is shocking enough to screenshot and share

### What makes it work:
- The opener FILTERS the audience — "people who don't hate me" means only fans engage positively, haters engage negatively. Both = engagement.
- "Literally" used twice — this is Bryce's verbal tic, makes it sound authentic
- The closer is offensive enough to go viral through outrage AND humor simultaneously
- Product is barely mentioned — the ATTITUDE is the viral element

### What's MISSING (for smarter drafts):
- No specific numbers (no $X, no timeframe)
- No product discovery moment — it's pure motivational
- No story, no character, no scenario
- Works bc Bryce is famous — would NOT work for a 5K follower account

### How to use this pattern:
- Use the polarizing opener structure ("to the people who X") for large, personality-driven accounts
- The "don't be [negative]" closer is a proven guilt trigger
- Works best when the influencer IS the brand — their attitude sells more than any product description
- Pair with specific numbers and a discovery moment to make it work for smaller accounts

## POST 2: Greentext/4chan Format — Self-Aware Meme
**Performance:** Viral (Cal-confirmed, 5M+ views)
**Format:** Greentext meme format (> arrows)

```
Be Higgsfield
< Realize AI Slop is going viral
< Use Claude Code to reverse engineer 5000+ of the most viral K-POP videos
< Copy Pasta prompt into Higgsfield
< Generate Zephyr (AI generated K-Pop goon slop)
< Get 1 million views in 1 hour on IG
< Profit.
```

### Psychology Analysis (18 Rules):
- **Rule 3 (Sarcasm > negation): ✅** "AI Slop" is self-aware sarcasm — acknowledging the criticism while embracing it
- **Rule 9 (5th grade semantics): ✅** Every concept is dead simple. Copy paste. Type. Get views. Profit.
- **Rule 16 (Mass virality): ✅** The greentext format is universally recognized. K-Pop + AI = mass appeal crossover.
- **Rule 17 (Funny/shocking): ✅** "AI generated K-Pop goon slop" is absurd enough to share. "1 million views in 1 hour" is jaw-dropping.
- **Rule 12 (Organic integration): ✅** Product is just one step in a larger hustle sequence — not the point of the post

### What makes it work:
- **Greentext is a PROVEN viral format** — people love the step-by-step "here's the exploit" structure
- **Self-aware about "slop"** — doesn't pretend AI content is high art. Leans into the absurdity.
- **Specific numbers:** 5000+ videos, 1 million views, 1 hour. Every line escalates.
- **"Profit."** — the one-word closer is the punchline. Classic greentext energy.
- **It's a RECIPE** — the reader sees themselves doing each step. That's the hook.
- **Multi-product mention** (Claude Code + Higgsfield) feels like a real workflow, not a single-product ad

### How to use this pattern:
- Greentext format works for meme accounts, tech accounts, degen accounts
- The "Be X / < Do Y / < Profit" structure is infinitely remixable
- Self-awareness about AI quality ("slop") makes the product mention feel honest, not shill-y
- Use escalating numbers at each step (small → bigger → absurd → profit)
- Works for meme wave AND ICP wave depending on the account
