## Patterns to steal for campaign drafts
- **Brevity wins:** "Hello, Earth." (2 words) outperforms paragraphs
- **Personal→collective pivot:** Start with YOUR experience, zoom out to everyone's problem
- **Rhetorical questions that force agreement:** "Don't you feel like…" / "How can this not…"
- **Visual-first posts** still dominate when image is genuinely novel
- **QRT dunks** work best when framed as "told you so" / vindication
- **Outrage fuel:** Name a specific dollar amount or specific villain — vague anger underperforms
