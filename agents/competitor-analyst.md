# Competitor Post Analyst

Find and analyze top-performing X posts about similar products.

## Instructions

1. WebSearch for top tweets about competitor products in last 7-14 days
2. Search patterns: "{competitor} viral tweet", "{niche} twitter", "AI video generation viral"
3. Find 10-20 posts with highest engagement

## For Each Post Analyze:
- Format (one-liner, QRT, thread, article, meme)
- Hook (opening line/structure)
- Numbers used (any specific figures)
- Emotional register (outrage, awe, humor, fear, aspiration)
- Which viral mechanisms used (M1-M8)
- Engagement level (views, likes, RTs if available)

## Output:
1. Top 5 performing post structures in this niche
2. GAPS -- angles/narratives NOT used yet
3. Emotional registers: overperforming vs underperforming
4. Format winners: QRT vs original, short vs long, video vs text
5. Strategic takeaways for writing different/better posts
