## 10 NARRATIVES WITH USE CASES

### Narrative 1: CGI Effects
**Angle:** VFX industry gatekeeping was about capital, not talent. Higgs eliminates the cost barrier.
**Numbers:**
- Single 6-second VFX combustion shot: $60K-$90K in Houdini, 3-5 weeks, 4-6 revision cycles of 8-14 hour renders
- Only studios with $15M-$40M VFX budgets could afford this
- Higgs: same shots from a text prompt in minutes. Each revision = another prompt, not another multi-day render
- A 22-year-old with a Higgs subscription = same capability as $40M VFX studio
**Service model:** Charge $5K per VFX shot (vs $60K-$90K old cost). 10/month = $50K/month.

### Narrative 2: First-Last Frame (FLF)
**Angle:** Upload first frame + last frame, describe what happens between. Storyboard becomes the film.
**Numbers:**
- Traditional animation pipeline: $12K-$25K for 14-second sequence, $150K-$300K per episode, $3M-$6M per season, 4-6 months per episode
- Gate was pipeline cost, not story quality. Tens of thousands of filmmakers have the story.
- Seedance 2.0: storyboard → film directly. Each drawing = first frame, next beat = last frame, everything between generated.
- Output: 8.2/10 benchmark, category leader by blind human preference
- Solo filmmaker with two images + Higgs subscription = output of studio with 47 specialists and $300K/episode budget

### Narrative 3: Extend + Interim Video (GAP CLOSURE SERVICE)
**Angle:** Every production has footage gaps they can't afford to reshoot. Turn gap closure into a $60K+/month service.
**Numbers:**
- Every production has 3-7 gaps (scenes that don't bridge, transitions, reaction shots never filmed)
- Reshoot cost: $15K-$40K per 90-second bridge + 6-8 weeks
- Seedance 2.0: bridge footage in minutes with character consistency and color grading match
- Service: charge $3K-$6K per gap (vs $15K-$40K reshoot). Client saves $9K-$34K per gap.
- Revenue: 5 gaps x 3 productions x $4K = $60K/month

### Narrative 4: Object Replace (FIX SERVICE)
**Angle:** VFX facilities charge $22K-$35K per object replacement. Build a fix service at $2K/shot.
**Numbers:**
- Object replacement (wrong car, logo clearance, background removal): $22K-$35K per fix, 8-12 days
- Full fix list quotes: $150K-$400K per production
- Seedance 2.0: same replacement from a prompt in minutes
- Service: charge $1,500-$3,000 per fix. Client saves $20K vs facility.
- Revenue: 15 fixes/month = $22.5K-$45K. 30 fixes/month = $45K-$90K.
- VFX facilities can't compete — their overhead is fixed. Your cost is a subscription.

### Narrative 5: Style Transfer
**Angle:** Previously impossible at any price. First movers own a market with zero competitors.
**Numbers:**

- No competitors exist because the capability didn't exist.
