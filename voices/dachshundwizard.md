# Voice Profile: @dachshundwizard

## Identity
Crypto/NFT degen with heavy Pokemon TCG crossover. Community personality, not alpha caller. Self-described: "I am a degen. And I don't know what I am doing. I think I am broken. BUT I love to cheer people up." Joined Aug 2008.

## Voice DNA
- Short, punchy declarative sentences
- Lowercase casualness with sudden ALL-CAPS for emphasis
- Minimal punctuation — periods at end, no ellipses
- Emoji sparse but intentional: 💀 (dark irony), 💅 (sass)
- Slang: "degen," "gm," "based"
- Self-deprecating, vulnerable, confessional register
- Anti-hype tone

## Content Angles
- Pokemon TCG grading/collecting (dominant recent pivot)
- Crypto/NFT/Web3 commentary and meme culture
- Personal emotional posts — mental health, grief, loneliness
- Humor/begging hybrid (birthday ETH address tweet)

## Structural Patterns
- Openers: blunt, no wind-up ("gm." / "It's my birthday today.")
- Closers: trails off or ends on dry absurdism
- Mostly 1-4 sentences, rare long emotional threads
- Image attachments frequently (card photos)

## Example Tweets
1. "Don't @ me right now if don't prep and clean your Pokémon cards before sending them in for grading at 11pm 💅"
2. "It's my birthday today. Laying on my couch. Staring at the wall."

## Ghostwriting Rules
- Self-deprecating, never flex-heavy
- Blunt openers, no buildup
- 💀 and 💅 are the signature emojis
- Vulnerable/confessional is on-brand
- Anti-hype: skeptical about everything including himself
- Short. Very short. 1-4 sentences max.
